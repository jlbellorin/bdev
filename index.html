About Me

In simple words I'm a 21yo Jr. Fullstack Developer, who is constantly learning and working hard to become better, since the beginning of my journey as a Web Developer I've focused on learning everything I can and gradually get knowledge on how to code in the backend and the frontend embracing the complicated but possible to keep with, road of the Fullstack, many would say that is difficult to be an expert on both ends of the web programming world but with hard work and experience is possible to have a pretty good level.

There's still a long way for me to achieve mastery but for sure I will work hard to give an excellent service and not let anyone down. If you'd like to contact me feel free to do it with the form on my website or through one of my social medias.
